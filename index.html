Code is Poetry

Web Development is an interesting discipline. It’s a place where the right and left brain collide to create something both technically precise and creative. I believe that function is what defines form; guiding it and sculpting it.
Music could be defined as one of the most emotive forms of creativity and yet without the structure of a key and tempo, music is just noise. It’s this balance in Web Development that fascinates me, it drives me toward creating something better.
